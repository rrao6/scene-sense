## Card 5 · Fact Check: Habeas Corpus?

**Cue point:** 00:46:46 → 00:47:16 (30s) · Scene 38

> *Paulette shouts "I'm taking the dog, dumbass!" just after Elle drops "habeas corpus" on Dewey.*

**On screen (playback overlay):**

```
[  Fact Check: Habeas Corpus?  ]
```

**Viewer taps → card expands:**

```
Could Elle really use habeas corpus to get the dog back?

Pure Hollywood bluff. Habeas corpus is a writ to produce a
detained person before a court — it has nothing to do with pets.
The correct legal action for recovering a dog is a writ of replevin.

[ Would her bluff actually work? ]   [ What's a writ of replevin? ]
```

**Viewer taps "Would her bluff actually work?" → follow-up becomes new title:**

```
Would her bluff actually work?

The tactic is real — lawyers call it dropping jargon on a
non-lawyer to close out a dispute before it reaches court.
Elle's version is just unusually fast.
```
