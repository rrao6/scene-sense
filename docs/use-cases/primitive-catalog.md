# UX primitive catalog

> Menu the content-intelligence layer picks from, and the menu design builds components for. **CTV pause is the MVP surface**; mobile, proactive, dual-screen, and send-to-phone are P2+.

## MVP (CTV pause) — what ships first

| Primitive | Monetizable? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **SceneIQ card** | ✅ (Tier A sponsored) | Scene-aware grounded fact; optional "See More" drawer |

## Grouping heuristics (from 2026-04-30 planning)

- **Franchise titles** — cameo alerts, character maps, sequel countdowns skew higher.
- **Nostalgia series** — Y2K gadget explainers, "where are they now?" cast, "Recognize them?" skew higher.
- **Historical / real-events** — "How real is it?", historical goofs, budget / production context.
- **Ensemble / procedural** — relationship maps, character-mapping polls.
- **Action / effects-heavy** — stunt-work explainers, budget facts.

## Legal sensitivity matrix (draft — confirm with Nicole Lodge)

| Primitive | Sensitivity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| SceneIQ (grounded fact) | Low | Cite source |
| Cast | Low | Avoid speculative personal claims |
| Trivia | Low | Must be verifiable |
| Poll (opinion) | Medium | No political / religious controversy at launch |
| Explainer | Medium | Medical / legal disclaimers required |
| "How real is it?" | Medium-High | Expert-framed vs. claim-making |
| Foreshadowing / spoilers | Medium | Opt-in UX, late-in-scene surfacing |
| Shoulder content | Inherits from source | Licensing review |
